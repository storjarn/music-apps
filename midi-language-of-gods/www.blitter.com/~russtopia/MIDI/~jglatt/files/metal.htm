Jump

Van Halen. Excellent demo of how to get the most convincing "overdriven guitar" sound out of a MIDI module. Check out how certain notes in the guitar solo, being played by a distorted guitar patch, are doubled by the guitar harmonics patch, in order to better simulate the natural feedback that a guitarist would get, at certain points, if he were playing with his amp turned up as loud as is done when playing this kind of music.
